about

I have never been completely satisfied with the look of my websites. In my efforts to improve what I don't like, I end up redesigning it - over, and over, and over...

That being said, simple, minimal sites have always appealed to me. Doing less somehow feels both calming and satisfying, and this version is my closest attempt so far.

The implementation is deliberately plain: static HTML and CSS, no JavaScript or framework, registered and hosted on Cloudflare.
